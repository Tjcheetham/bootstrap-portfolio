About

My name's TJ and I'm from Montana! My wife and I moved to Scottsdale, Arizona four years ago and we love it! After living most of my life in the colder northern regions, the heat has been a welcome reprieve from months of cold and snow. I'll take the furnace over the freezer any day!

I'm a big fan of pretty much all sports but I specifically enjoy playing basketball, golf, and sand volleyball. I also follow NFL football pretty closely where I also enjoy beating my friends in fantasy football.

To throw a curve ball in to the whole thing, I swam competitively for almost 10 years and parlayed that into my current career as the South Lead Age Group Coach & Site Director for Scottsdale Aquatic Club (SAC). I teach primarily 12 & Unders and really enjoy what I do! Above all, I just love learning and am currently enjoying my new passion learning to code as a student in the University of Arizona's Full Stack Web Development program! I'm looking forward to the challenges that the future holds as I continue to expand my coding knowledge.
